About Us


We are a lifeline when someone disappears


help4u is independent and purely a work of charity in India which is dedicated to bringing missing children and adults back together with their families. Some missing people you will have heard of, but many more you won’t. For their families, life can feel like a desperate and unbearable struggle as they wait for days or even years. That’s why we are here for missing people and their families, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, to provide free and confidential support.

We are caring, skilled staff and volunteers working around the clock. We offer a lifeline for nearly 250,000 people who run away and go missing each year. We listen in confidence, and support missing people and their families to explore their options and, where possible, to reconnect. For those left behind, we provide specialised support to ease the heartache and confusion, and help search for their missing loved ones. We make every effort to ensure that the support and information we provide is accurate and appropriate. We also signpost to other Non Governmental Organization as part of the services we offer and we always strive to ensure that the referrals we make are appropriate and to reputable organisations. However we cannot accept liability for the actions of any other services we may signpost you to.

Our vision is that every missing person is found safe. We are here to ensure that anyone who is missing can find a safe way to reconnect with a loved one, to get a message home, or, to get to a place of safety and that families are supported whilst they live with not knowing what has happened to someone they love.

This web application is divided into following parts:-

  • An individual who can register themselves :

    • Any individual can register themselves in our site by providing their details. They can further complain about any missing person or can register as volunteers to help us. They can also view the timeline where the missing people are accounted and can comment if they have any information about any of them.

  • An individual who can register complaint of missing person :

    • One can complain about the missing people by giving their full or known details. They can get both comments as notification on their missing people complain and can also comment on other people's missing people complaint.

  • Any NGO who can register themselves for the cause :

    • Any valid non-governmental organisation can register themselves by giving their valid information. No false declarations and information will be certified. This non-governmental organisation will be further verified by certified volunteers and get validated by admin. When validated, any non-governmental organisation can take up the task to find any missing people and serve their cause to bring families together. Our site works as a bridge between these non-governmental organisation's and missing people's families.

  • An individual who can volunteer to the cause :

    • One eager to help can always volunteer in our site. This volunteer will be certified by the admin only when their given details are verified by other existing volunteers. These individuals will receive the tasks by the admin.
