Help Us to Find Someone


Our vision is that every missing person is found safe. We are here to ensure that anyone who is missing can find a safe way to reconnect with a loved one, to get a message home, or, to get to a place of safety and that families are supported whilst they live with not knowing what has happened to someone they love.

You can always help us to find the missing peoples by registering yourself as an user. As an user, you can comment and provide any information on other missing peoples' picture shown to you in the timeline.

Further, you can also apply to volunteer in our site.

If you are a valid NGO, you can also register yourself and help us to find those missing and bring them back to their families and loved ones.
