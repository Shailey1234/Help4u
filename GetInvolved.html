Get Involved


As Users

All the user can get involved with us by registering themselves. They can comment on the pictures of the missing peoples shown in the timeline. They can complain about any missing people and also apply to be a volunteer in our site and help us.

As Volunteers

All the volunteers can get involved by applying for volunteership. They can perform the tasks given to them by the admin only after they are validated by the admin.
